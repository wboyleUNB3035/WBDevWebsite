VariousRPi Projects

Hardware Exploration

Since I first purchased my Raspberry Pi 4 (and Arduino UNO!) back in May 2020, I've created multiple small-scale projects to explore the capabilities of the small microcomputer and electronics.

Some examples of my projects include the RetroPi, PiHole, Kali Linux Sandbox, and a live reptile terrarium camera. I've also tinkered with the Arduino Uno to explore what can be possible with the small-but-mighty microcontroller.

I've really enjoyed tinkering with these these tiny computers because they've exposed me to a broader range of things that are possible in the Computer Science world. And it's also just very fun to make short and sweet weekend projects :)
