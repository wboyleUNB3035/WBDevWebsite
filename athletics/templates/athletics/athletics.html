My Story:

I'm a high performance Cross Country and Track athlete born in Winnipeg, Manitoba. I currently compete for the University of New Brunswick, based in Fredericton, NB. Additionally, I compete for the Saint John REDS Track and Field Club.

I primarily compete in events from the 800m up to the 10,000m. However, I specialize in the 1500m, 3000m Steeplechase, and the 5000m. I've run competitively since 8th grade and I hope to continue competing post collegiately.
